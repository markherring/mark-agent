## Instructions for Use

**When populating this template:**

1. **Be factual in the company sections** (Overview, Product, Market Position, Competitive Landscape, Leadership)
2. **Be honest in the process sections** (Role Target, Interview Log, Problems)
3. **Be authentic in the "Why Mark is Excited" section** - this is what makes the agent sound like YOU
4. **Be specific in talking points** - reference actual company details, not generic statements
5. **Remove any section that doesn't apply** - not every company needs every section
6. **Remove all instruction blocks** (like this one) when finished

**What makes a good company file:**
- ✅ Specific details about their tech/product
- ✅ Your genuine excitement with concrete reasons
- ✅ Problems you'd solve that tie to your experience
- ✅ Talking points that sound like you, not a bot
- ❌ Generic statements that could apply to any company
- ❌ Made-up information or exaggerated claims
- ❌ Process details you wouldn't want them to see (if you plan to share this agent)
